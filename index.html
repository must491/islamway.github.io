The 5 Fard prayers

Fard prayers are the prayers that are mandatory, every muslim needs to pray them

  • 1. Fajr (the morning prayer that has 2 rakats)
  • 2. Duhr (the afternoon prayer that has 4 rakats)
  • 3. Asr (the late afternoon prayer that has 4 rakats)
  • 4. Maghrib (the sunset prayer that has 3 rakats)
  • 5. Isha (the midnight prayer that has 4 rakats)

    The wajib prayer are the obligatory specific situation prayer that is special include

  • the funeral prayer
  • the eid prayer
  • the tawaf prayer
  • the jumah prayer
  • the witr prayer

The sunnah prayer are extra... but this is not true... the sunnah is really really heavily empathasized and to be honest you should just consider them as mandatory prayer as well.

The 5 pillars of Islam

  • testifying allah is one and that Muhammad is the messenger (shahadda)
  • submiting prayers
  • giving charity
  • performing the pilgrimage (Hajj)
  • fasting during the month of Ramadan

The 5 pillars of islam are the outward actions that show and summarize how you submit to allah

The 6 pillars of Iman

  • belief in allah (that he is one and the absolute creator of everything)
  • belief in the angels ( that they are real servants of allah)
  • belief in the books (torah, bible, psamls, gospel of davud, scrolls of ibrahim, and the quran)
  • belief in his messengers(all of the 25 prophets mentioned in the quran and the other 124,000 )
  • belief in the day of judgment (that you will be held acountable and there is heaven and hell)
  • belief in divine decree (that everything, good or bad, happens by allah's will)

The pillars of Iman are the inward actions that show you belief and summarize how you think and trust of Islam
